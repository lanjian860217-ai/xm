No, unfortunately you cant change the time zone on your MT4 or MT5 trading terminal.

Our servers are always set to GMT+2 during winter and GMT+3 during summer. Adopting the GMT time zone helps avoid having small candlesticks on Sundays, allowing you to conduct smoother, more straightforward technical analysis and back-testing.

No, we wont close your open positions or pending orders if you go offline. They will remain on the platform and appear the next time you log in.

This applies for all order types except trailing stops, which become inactive when you go offline or log out of MT4 or MT5. Expert Advisors (EAs) also become inactive on the platforms when you log out or go offline.
